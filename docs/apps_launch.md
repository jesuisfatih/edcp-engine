partnered with.

#### Built for Shopify certification

The Built for Shopify status and badge gives apps a significant competitive
edge in the Shopify App Store.

[How to qualify](https://shopify.dev/built-for-shopify)

#### Profit on the Shopify App Store

0% revenue share on the first $1,000,000 USD in annual gross app revenue
earned through the Shopify App Store.
